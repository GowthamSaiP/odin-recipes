Vegetable Biryani

Description

Vegetable Biryani is an aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, mix veggies, herbs & biryani spices. This easy Veg Biryani is a total crowd pleaser! You will love this for everything from busy night meals to holiday family dinners.

Ingredients

    for marination

  • 1 cup curd.
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste.
  • 2 chilli, slit.
  • 2 tsp lemon juice.
  • 1 pack shan biryani masala.
  • ½ tsp salt.
  • 1 potato, cubed.
  • 1 carrot, chopped.
  • ½ capsicum, cubed.
  • 5 beans, chopped.
  • 3 tbsp coriander, chopped.
  • 3 tbsp mint, chopped.

    • for biryani gravy

  • 2 tbsp ghee.
  • 2 tbsp oil.
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste.
  • 1 onion, finely chopped.
  • 2 tbsp fried onion / barista.

    • for biryani rice

  • 2 cup basmati rice.
  • water, for soaking and boiling.
  • 2 bay leaf.
  • 2 inch cinnamon.
  • 1 pod pod black cardamom.
  • 2 pods cardamom.
  • 1 javitri.
  • 6 cloves.
  • 1 star anise.
  • 2 tsp ghee.
  • 1 tsp lemon juice.
  • 1 tsp cumin.
  • ½ tsp pepper.
  • 2 tsp salt.

    • for layering

  • Banana leaf.
  • 2 tbsp saffron milk.
  • 1 tbsp mint, chopped.
  • 1 tbsp coriander, chopped.
  • 1 tsp ghee.
  • Biryani masala.

Steps

1. Marinate vegetables

  1. Firstly, in a large bowl take 1 cup curd, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste, 2 chilli, 2 tsp lemon juice, 1 pack shan biryani masala and ½ tsp salt.
  2. Mix well combining all the ingredients well.
  3. Add 1 potato, 1 carrot, ½ capsicum, 5 beans, 3 tbsp coriander and 3 tbsp mint.
  4. Cover and marinate for 30 minutes or more.

2. Make veg biryani gravy

  1. Firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp ghee and 2 tbsp oil.
  2. Saute 1 onion, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste and saute well.
  3. Saute until the onions turn golden brown.
  4. Now add marinated vegetables and cook until the oil separates.
  5. Once the vegetables are almost cooked add 2 tbsp fried onion and mix well.
  6. Biryani base is ready.

3. Make biryani rice

  1. Firstly, rinse and soak 2 cups of basmati rice for 20 minutes.
  2. In a large vessel take enough water.
  3. Also add 2 bay leaf, 2 inch cinnamon, 1 pod black cardamom, 2 pods cardamom, 1 javitri, 6 cloves and 1 star anise.
  4. Stir and mix well again.
  5. Further add 2 tsp ghee, 1 tsp lemon juice, 1 tsp cumin, ½ tsp pepper and 2 tsp salt.
  6. Mix well and get to a rolling boil.
  7. Add in soaked rice and mix well.
  8. Boil the rice until it is almost cooked.
  9. Drain off the rice and keep aside.
  10. Firstly, in a banana leaf spread a ladleful of prepared biryani gravy.
  11. Add in almost cooked basmati rice.
  12. Then pour 2 tbsp saffron milk, 1 tbsp mint, 1 tbsp coriander, 1 tsp ghee and sprinkle biryani masala.
  13. Cover and seal using a toothpick.
  14. Now place the banana leaf into the steamer and steam for 30 minutes.
  15. Finally, enjoy veg biriyani recipe with raita and salan.
